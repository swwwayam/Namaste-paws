About Namaste Paws

Namaste Paws is an initiative by Swayam Ballal and Ansh Kudalkar From Vidyalankar Institute Of Technology dedicated to helping abandoned and rescued animals find loving homes in India. We partner with local shelters and connect potential adopters with their perfect furry companions. Our mission is to promote responsible pet ownership and the well-being of animals in our community. We believe that every pet deserves a loving family and every family deserves the joy of having a pet. Through our platform, we aim to make the adoption process easier and more accessible.

Thank you for considering adoption and giving a deserving animal a second chance!
